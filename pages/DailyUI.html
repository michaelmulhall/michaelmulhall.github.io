DailyUI


Challenging myself to design something every day for 100 days while expanding my creativity by exploring various UI trends.

Wanting to apply my skills practically, each design is crafted for an existing company. Treating each DailyUI as a client meant I got to learn about and explore the branding guidelines of a new company each day.

Tools:
Adobe XD, Figma, Illustrator, Photoshop, Procreate

Timeframe:
100 days (Summer 2020)

DailyUI - 003

Landing page

A modern and exquisit landing page to match the aesthetics that a Adobe software possesses.

DailyUI - 008

404 error page

A play on being lost in the woods and source not being found, for North Face.
