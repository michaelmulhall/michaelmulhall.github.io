A bit more about me



You’ve traveled a long way, catch a breath, and allow me to introduce myself! My name is Michael Mulhall, I am a designer who loves to tinker at complex business problems in ambiguous environments and derive solutions using design thinking. I'm a passionate product + ux designer. Some things I'm really into are visual design, software development, AI, functional programming, user interface design, and graphic design.

I have always been very detail-oriented, and I make conscious efforts to understand causes instead of just the effects. I love problem-solving in an orderly manner to find the solution. I am an active team player who is able to multitask. Additionally, I have always had the ability to grasp multiple technologies, and consistently strive to exceed expectations.



So… Why do I design?



My passion for design developed when I was a little kid. I always loved drawing, as it was my go-to mood booster. This eventually became more than just a passion when I discovered the field of HCI.

The industry allows me to continuously develop my interests; whether that be honing my visual craft, understanding the jargon of code, or bringing diversified perspectives to the table.
